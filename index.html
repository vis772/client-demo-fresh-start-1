FAQs

Quick answers to common questions.

How long does ceramic coating last?

We offer options from 1 year up to multi‑year systems. Longevity depends on maintenance and exposure.

Is PPF better than ceramic?

They do different jobs: PPF protects from impact and scratches, ceramic adds gloss and makes cleaning easier. Many choose both.

What tint shades do you offer?

A range from light to dark, with legal-compliant options. We’ll guide you to the best balance of privacy and visibility.
